llm_quality_score_filter¶

Filter to keep samples with a high quality score estimated by a language model.

This operator uses a language model to evaluate the quality of each sample across multiple dimensions, including accuracy, grammar, informativeness, and coherence. The LLM provides a numerical score for each dimension on a 1-5 scale, where 1 is the lowest and 5 is the highest. The overall quality score is used to decide whether to keep or filter out the sample based on the specified minimum and maximum score thresholds. The evaluation results are cached in the 'llm_quality_score' and 'llm_quality_record' fields. Important flags and tags from the LLM's analysis may also be stored in the sample's stats.

用于保留由语言模型估计出高质量分数的样本的过滤器。

该算子使用语言模型评估每个样本在多个维度上的质量，包括准确性、语法、信息量和连贯性。LLM为每个维度提供一个1-5分的数值评分，其中1分最低，5分最高。总体质量分数用于根据指定的最小和最大分数阈值来决定是否保留或过滤掉样本。评估结果缓存在'llm_quality_score'和'llm_quality_record'字段中。LLM分析的重要标志和标签也可能存储在样本的stats中。

Type 算子类型: filter

Tags 标签: cpu, vllm, hf, api

✨ explanation 解释¶

This example demonstrates the LLMQualityScoreFilter on a dataset of three sentences. The operator evaluates each sentence and assigns a quality score based on factors like grammar, coherence, and informativeness. Sentences with higher scores are kept, while those with lower scores are filtered out. In this case, the first sentence, which is grammatically incorrect and nonsensical, receives the lowest score and is removed. The other two sentences, which are more coherent and informative, receive higher scores and are retained. 这个例子展示了LLMQualityScoreFilter在一个包含三个句子的数据集上的应用。算子根据语法、连贯性和信息量等因素对每个句子进行评估，并分配一个质量分数。得分较高的句子被保留，而得分较低的句子则被过滤掉。在这个例子中，第一个句子由于语法错误且无意义，得分最低并被移除。其他两个句子更加连贯和有信息量，得分较高并被保留。

✨ explanation 解释¶

This example shows how the LLMQualityScoreFilter works on a dataset where each sample contains a question, an analysis, and an answer. The operator evaluates the quality of the entire sample, considering the text, analysis, and answer together. Samples with higher quality scores are kept, while those with lower scores are filtered out. In this case, all three samples are evaluated, and the one with the highest quality score is retained. 这个例子展示了LLMQualityScoreFilter在一个每个样本包含问题、分析和答案的数据集上的应用。算子会综合考虑文本、分析和答案来评估整个样本的质量。质量分数较高的样本被保留，而分数较低的样本则被过滤掉。在这个例子中，所有三个样本都被评估，质量分数最高的样本被保留。
